About

Welcome to the Auburn University at Montgomery Computer Science website! Here you will learn what this website has to offer as well as all the information you need to keep up to date on our services and projects. You will learn what Computer Science entails so that you can better make a decision for a career in the CS field.

Striving Goals

  • We aim to make Computer Science as readily accessible and flexible as possible.
  • We plan to prepare and ready our students as much as possible for as many jobs in the CS field as we can.

Important News and Events

The new semester starts soon! Don't delay in picking your classes! You don't want to be stuck with an 8:00 AM class!

The end of the semester is fast approaching. Keep your physical and mental state intact and don't overwork yourself too hard. We want you to succeed! That being said, there has been a recent uptick in the use of websites that sell or allow the viewership of answers to test or assignment questions. Please note that any violation of the academic honesty policy is subject to consequences. Doing your work honestly is the best way to learn and retain what you learn. Remember, we want you to succeed!

We want to hear YOUR opinion on the College of Sciences' courses and professors! In order to keep improving, we must know how we are catering to students and what can be done to increase the effectiveness of our services. The only way to know that is through YOU, the student! Go to the Curtiss Critique tab in BlackBoard or on the MyAUM site to get started.
